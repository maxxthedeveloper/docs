## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [focuh.com](https://focuh.com) and sign up with your Google account or email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first task">
    Click the **Add Task** button to create your first task. Give it a name and optionally set a duration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a focus session">
    Click on a task and press the **Focus** button to start a timed focus session. Stay focused and complete your task!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Install the Desktop App

The Focuh desktop app blocks distracting websites while you're in a focus session.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Download the Focuh desktop app from the [download page](/download).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Open the app and sign in with your Focuh account.
  </Step>
