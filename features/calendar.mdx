Connect your Google Calendar to see your events alongside your tasks in Focuh.

## Connecting Google Calendar

1. Go to **Settings** in Focuh
2. Click **Connect Google Calendar**
3. Authorize Focuh to access your calendar
4. Your events will appear in the calendar view

## Features

### See Events Alongside Tasks

Your calendar events appear in the time grid, so you can plan your tasks around meetings and appointments.

### Focus6 Calendar

Focuh can create a dedicated "Focus6" calendar in your Google account to track your focus sessions as events.

<Tip>
This is useful for seeing your focus time in your regular calendar apps and for time tracking purposes.
</Tip>

## Privacy

Focuh only reads your calendar events to display them. We don't store your calendar data on our servers.
